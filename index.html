This is the demo page for Learning Emotional Representations from Imbalanced Speech Data for Speech Emotion Recognition and Emotional Text-to-Speech

Emotion Expressiveness Test

The models should mimic the speaking style of the reference, and express proper emotions. All models are trained from an imbalanced dataset, where each speaker only has 10 samples for each emotion class except Neutral class.

Baseline: Lee, Younggun and Taesu Kim. "Robust and Fine-grained Prosody Control of End-to-end Speech Synthesis." ICASSP 2019.
